About OpenBook

OpenBook is a chill, community-powered digital library curated and maintained by Dwip 💻📚

This project started as a 9th-grade idea — to build a site where anyone could download free books. Now, it's something real: a centralized hub to access some of the best free PDFs on the internet.

📖 The idea hit me during an educational tour to a nearby slum area. I saw kids who had dreams but no resources — no books, no guidance. That moment stuck with me. So I created OpenBook to make knowledge more accessible — for them, and for anyone who needs it.

⚠️ Disclaimer: OpenBook does not host pirated content. All files are either in the public domain, educational, or freely available online. The goal is to help people learn, grow, and explore knowledge — without ads, logins, or shady popups 💀.

🚀 Built with love using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Hosted on Netlify. Updated manually because I like pain 😎
